Context: The Processor Memory. Every abstract hardware type will have specific implementation types as media constructs to represent the context (of these abstract types). As for navigational access of the abstract conceptual type? The implementation types are meant to build up to the idea of random-access separators as navigators, specifically sifters/filters. Such accessors have highest entropy, but are also the computationally most complex. The first step in that direction but of simpler computational efficiency are the types that are naturally associated with iterators. The exception to the rule are builtin types. As they do not support their own iterators or separators. They get their own categorization as builtin.
